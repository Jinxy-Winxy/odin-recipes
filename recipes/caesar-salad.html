Caesar Salad

Homepage

Description

This is a simple 20 minute meal that tastes yummy with only a few ingredients, this makes a meal for two people

Ingredients

  • 400 g lettuce
  • 2 tbsp caesar salad sauce mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • 40 g Parmesan cheese
  • 400g chicken breast
  • Optional - handful of crouton

Steps

  1. Wash the lettuce and then rip into chunks and put into a big bowl
  2. Then add 2 tbsp of caesar salad sauce with 2 tbsp of water
  3. Add 40 g parmesan cheese to the bowel of lettuce
  4. Give it a good mix
  5. Add salt and pepper to chicken breast and cook at medium heat for 3 mins each size until cooked
  6. Once the chicken has been cooked, cut into bite size chunks and add to the lettuce
  7. Then the meal can be served
