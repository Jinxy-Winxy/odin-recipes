Pancakes

Homepage

Description

This recipe will be able to make six fluffy pancakes, only need 5 ingredients and it will take 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 300g Flour
  • 2 Egg
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla
  • 400ml Milk
  • 30 g Protein powder
  • 1 cal spray oil
  • Maple syrup topping

Steps

  1. Add the wet ingredients to the blender
  2. Add dry ingredients and blend until smooth
  3. Add 5 squirts spray oil onto pan at medium heat
  4. Add small size of mixture to pan depending on how big you want your pancakes
  5. Cook for 2 mins and lip
  6. Once cooked remove and add to a plate
  7. Repeat until all mixture is done
