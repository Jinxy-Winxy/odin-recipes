Description

This recipe will show you how to make a high calories filling breakfast with a good amount of fibre

Ingredients

  • 100g of oats
  • 250ml of whole milk
  • 30g chia seeds
  • 30g honey
  • 10g coco powder

Steps

  1. Get a bowl with a lid and add the dry ingredients
  2. Then add the wet ingredients and add the lid
  3. Give the mixture a good shake for 60 seconds then taste
  4. Leave if the fridge for 10 to 12 hours before eating
  5. Optional - add berries or banana when served
